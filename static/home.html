About Us

At EmpoweRRRs, we connect restaurants with shelters and organizations to reduce food waste and fight hunger. By redistributing surplus food to those in need, we help minimize waste while supporting the homeless and low-income families in Worcester. Our platform tracks and alerts users about available food, ensuring efficient distribution to shelters and individuals facing food insecurity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to combat food waste and hunger by connecting food services with shelters and organizations that serve the homeless and low-income communities. We aim to ensure that surplus food is efficiently redistributed, preventing waste and providing nutritious meals to those in need.
